Pricing

With Guidely audio tour system, you have a choice of 2 plans – pay as you go or monthly plan. Both plans have a free tier.

In this plan the price you pay is dependent on the number of exhibits you have in your collection:

Pay as you go

In this plan, you pay only for what you use. You are charged based on the number of audio tracks is being played.

The Guidely free tier includes 500 free plays of audio track per month:

  • First, 500 free audio track plays per month are free
  • €0.0001 (one tenth of Eurocent) per single audio track play thereafter

Pricing example 1

Your gallery is visited by 10 people daily, 50% of them decides to use audio guide system, your exposition has 20 locations with audio guide, see total monthly cost:

Total monthly charges = (5 visitors * 30 days * 20  locations – 500 free tier audio plays) * €0.0001 = €0.20 (20 Eurocents)

Pricing example 2

Your gallery is visited by 50 people daily, 50% of them decides to use audio guide system, your exposition has 100 locations with audio guide, see total monthly cost:

Total monthly charges = (25 visitors * 30 days * 100 locations 500 free tier audio plays) * €0.0001 = €7.5

For every new customer, Guidely provides a 30-day money back guarantee.

 

Recording services

The session costs €20, it includes recording session in your selected location using our professional microphone and sound post-processing.
