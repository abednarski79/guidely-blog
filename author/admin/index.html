Text-to-speech

February 7, 2017February 10, 2017 ABLeave a comment

New feature text-to-speech allows for creating audio guide without the need of recording the narration.

Guidly uses high quality device built in voice – Siri in iPhone and Google Voice in Android.

This feature allows for quick and easy editing of the content.

Loading of text-to-speech based audio guide is instant and use very little bandwidth on user device.

Example of text-to-speech guide:

http://guidely.appbucket.eu/guide/1/location/select

Enjoy.

Smartphone as media of information for the exhibition

December 19, 2016December 20, 2016 ABLeave a comment

These days increasing number of museums accepts or even encourages visitors to use the smartphone.

There are few great things which could be used when this type of technology is accepted in public spaces:

  • visitors smartphones can be used to promote exhibition in social media by the guest himself – for example she or he can click on social link under the location audio track presented on Guidely web application
  • owner of the can get statistics about which of the present objects are most interesting for the visitors or path which was choose by guests

More information about mobile enable museum can be found at “Please Turn On Your Phone in the Museum”
